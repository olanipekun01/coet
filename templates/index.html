Welcome to the College of Engineering & Technology

Welcome to the College of Engineering and Technology (COET) at Achievers University, Owo—a hub for innovation, creativity, and excellence in engineering education. Since its establishment in 2017, the College has been committed to producing world-class engineers equipped with the knowledge, skills, and entrepreneurial mindset necessary to tackle the challenges of the 21st century.

With cutting-edge programmes in Biomedical Engineering, Civil and Environmental Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Mechatronics Engineering, we integrate theory and practice to ensure our graduates excel both in academia and industry. Guided by the university's vision to be the best in Africa and among the world’s finest, the College fosters an enabling environment for academic excellence, practical learning, and research-driven innovation.

Whether your ambition is to advance technology, build sustainable infrastructures, or revolutionize industries, the College of Engineering and Technology is your pathway to a fulfilling career in engineering.
